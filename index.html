What are fake news?

Fake news are misleading or totally fabricated information that mimics the form of mainstream news.

Are fake news created and spread by purpose?

Absolutely yes! They are purposefully crafted, sensational and emotionally charged.

Details of Fake News

Why and How

Q: What are the intention of creating fake news?
A: They are fabricated to influence polictical views or to serve as a joke.

Q: How are fake news spread?
A: They are usually spread on the Internet or via social medias, due to transfer of gatekeeping role from traditional journalists to the entire Net users.

Q: How are fake news determined and controlled?
A: It is time-consuming and complicated to define fake news as different governments or authorities take different approach. There are no distinct guidelines for judging what fake news are, making it difficult to be regulated.
